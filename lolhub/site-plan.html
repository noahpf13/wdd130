Purpose

This site serves as a personal League of Legends improvement hub where I can track my ranked progress, organize gameplay notes, and save helpful resources as I climb.

Audience

The intended audience is myself and potentially other League players interested in structured personal improvement and consistent ranked progress.

Content

Home page

Hi, I'm Killamas. I want to get better at League. This site is how going to keep track of my climb to Gold. Some say League might be the hardest games to play currently. In my experience, I have never gone higher then Silver 4 on ADC. Skilling up may take more than a casual effort. So, I want this page to be a resource for anyone who is looking for inspiration on how others use a more goal-oriented strategy to improve at LoL.

Images for the Home page

The Climb

The Climb will be where I track my ranked progression, champions played, and key takeaways from my games, making it easy to see improvement over time. By logging sessions and reflections, I will identify patterns, measure progress, and stay motivated I work toward my League goals.

Images for the Page 2

ChatReadThis

ChatReadThis is my space for saving useful notes, screenshots, and advice I find on Reddit, YouTube, or Twitch so you can revisit important tips and strategies. Whether it’s a macro strategy, mental reminder, or a clip to review, this page keeps mylearning organized in one place for quick reference.
