This page holds all the tips, reminders, and screenshots I find helpful from Twitch, Reddit, YouTube, and friends.

“Don’t chase kills in mid game unless you're ahead—focus on objectives and vision.” – r/summonerschool

Reddit

  • Rotation advice: "Back when ahead, reset, group."
  • Freeze lane if behind. Avoid solo pushing.
  • Mute chat, not pings.

Twitch

  • Pro tip: Use your minimap like a radar.
  • Set camera to semi-lock for faster tracking.
  • Watch pro VODs at x1.25 speed.
