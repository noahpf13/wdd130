I want to get better at League of Legends.

This site is how I'm going to keep track of my climb to Gold. Some say League might be one of the hardest games to play currently. In my experience, I’ve never gone higher than Silver 4 on ADC. Skilling up may take more than a casual effort. So I want this page to be a resource for others who want to improve using a goal-oriented strategy.

What Is My Goal?

Follow my progress as I set goals, review my gameplay, and track wins and losses in the climb to Gold. Learn from what works and what doesn’t—updated regularly with stats and thoughts from every ranked session.
