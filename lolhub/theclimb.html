Understanding Ranked Tiers

In League of Legends, ranked gameplay is divided into several tiers that represent a player's skill level. Each tier (except the top three) has four divisions, from IV (lowest) to I (highest).

The ranked tiers are:
Iron, Bronze, Silver, Gold, Platinum, Emerald, Diamond, Master, Grandmaster, and Challenger.
